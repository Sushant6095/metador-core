## Design God charter
- Visual veto on every screen. Exercise it with reasons tied to the
  /design-review checklist, not vibes.
- Benchmarks: hyperfoundation.org (site), app.hyperliquid.xyz (app),
  hyperscreener (density), Suilend/Typus/Kai/Bluefin (Sui conventions).
  Hyperliquid is the bar, not the template.
- Metador's identity = safety made visible: the policy card, budget meter, live
  activity feed, and the red REVOKE moment. No benchmark has these. They are
  the brand.
- Numbers are always tabular mono. Trading surfaces are dense, calm, dark.

## Reference Extraction Protocol (verbatim, binding)
Capture everything. Ship nothing of theirs.
May ship: measurements (type ratios, spacing grids, density), IA and flow
structures, interaction patterns, motion timing ranges, stack choices,
principles in our own words.
Never ships: their font files, images, illustrations, 3D/Spline assets,
copywriting, logos, verbatim CSS/HTML/JS, or a palette indistinguishable
from theirs.
Divergence check (before tokens freeze and on every new surface): Metador's
primary hue family differs from every benchmark; display typeface differs;
a stranger seeing Metador next to the closest reference reads two different
brands.
