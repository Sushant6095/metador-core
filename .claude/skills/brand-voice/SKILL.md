# Brand Voice

## The voice
Calm engineer who has nothing to hide. Short declaratives. Concrete nouns.
We explain custody the way a locksmith explains a lock — physical, checkable,
no adjectives doing load-bearing work.

## Core vocabulary (use these exact frames)
- "Limits enforced by the chain, not by trust."
- "The operator's key can trade. It cannot withdraw. Not 'is not allowed' —
  cannot."
- "Funds cannot be stolen; losses are capped by the ceiling you wrote."
- "Revoke kills it instantly. Withdraw is always yours."
- Walls, ceiling, mandate, locked — our metaphor family is physical
  structure. Never magic, never "trustless" as a vibe word without the
  mechanism in the same sentence.

## Hard rules (claim discipline — legal surface)
- NEVER: earnings promises, APY projections, "passive income," "can't lose
  money," "guaranteed," "risk-free."
- Risk disclosure before first deposit, in voice (plain, short), not pasted
  legalese.
- Every on-chain claim must be checkable: pair it with the explorer link.
- Jargon policy: plain words first, term in parens once per surface —
  "the vault's trading key (TradeCap)".

## Microcopy patterns
- Errors: what happened + what to do next. "The chain refused: this order
  would exceed the vault's budget ceiling." Never raw codes alone.
- Buttons: verbs, not nouns ("Deposit", "Revoke vault", "Place order").
- Empty states sell the next action ("Be the first leader on Metador").

## Anti-patterns
Exclamation marks. "Revolutionary/seamless/supercharge." Fear-mongering
competitors by name in-product (the demo script may cite public incidents
factually, with sources). Emoji in product surfaces.
